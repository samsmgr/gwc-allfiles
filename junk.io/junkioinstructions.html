Hi, we are glad that you joined junk.io. The purpose of the game is to collect all the food and avoid the trash in the water. If you eat the trash, you lose health. By collecting all the food you may proceed. After every level, a fact about the codition of our Earth will appear. To play you press ENTER. To go forward press the RIGHT arrow. To go backwards press the LEFT arrow. To go up you use the UP arrow. To go up press the UP arrow. To go down press the DOWN arrow. To reset, press the reset button located at the top of the screen.
